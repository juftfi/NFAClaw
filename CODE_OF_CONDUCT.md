# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

We expect contributors to:
- Focus on technical facts and reproducible evidence.
- Avoid harassment, abuse, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Respect maintainers' and contributors' time.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Threats or intimidation
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy, and may limit participation for repeated violations.

## Reporting
If you observe behavior violating this code, report it privately to repository maintainers.
